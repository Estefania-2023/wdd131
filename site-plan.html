Pearl World Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

This website is related to the sale of the products. The name of the website could be "Pearl World". The products are crafts made of pearls, for example: animals made of pearls, mats, decorations, etc. The purpose of creating this website is to show and guide people interested in finding new things about crafting and can buy anything they want. They can use these products to decorate their room, kitchen, living room, and anywhere in their house.

Audience

The audience could be all the people who are interesting and motivated to buy products for decoration in their room or house.

Content or Scenario

Home page

To start, this is a website related to the sale of products. I called Pearl World because there are products that are crafts made of pearls, for example, animals made of pearls, mats, decorations, etc. I know this website is a nice place where many people who are interested in finding new things about crafting, and they can buy anything they want. They can use these products to decorate their room, kitchen, living room, and anywhere in their house. The questions that they could ask are: “Is this website the correct place to find interesting things? Are the product’s prices comfortable? What product is the most sold and with the good quality? Are these products as decorating for my room or anywhere in the house? Can I use some of these products as gifts for familiar ones on Christmas? Also, can I use one of them as a key ring? How can I know this is true?” I will respond: “Here are many products with excellent quality, and with good and comfortable prices, including free delivery. In other words, everybody is welcome to buy anything you want. Here are the necessary things you need to decorate your room kitchen, living room, and anywhere in your house; also, it can be a little decoration in your office or in the place where you are working. Moreover, you can use them as gifts for all the people you love, this is a wonderful idea to make your relation more stronger with them. And yes, you can use it as a key ring or a decoration that you are bringing in your backpack, your bag, etc. In addition to this presentation, I made this website with effort and love of mine, because I want to bring you these helpful products that could be amazing in your life, and I want to guide you to find these things you need. These products could help you at any moment until it could make your relation more strongest with your family and friends. This is the opportunity to advantage of these beautiful products”
